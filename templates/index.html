About Resource Allocation Manager (RAM)

The Resource Allocation Manager (RAM) is designed to optimize the distribution of essential resources during flood disasters.

  • Real-time weather data integration for accurate flood risk assessment.
  • Severity analysis based on location elevation and rainfall data.
  • Efficient resource allocation to affected areas to minimize impact.

RAM aims to provide a systematic approach to disaster management, ensuring that resources are directed to the areas most in need, reducing the overall damage and aiding in quicker recovery.
